The **Milton Keynes ECF/FIDE Chess Congress 2026** takes place on **<time datetime="2026-03-28">Saturday 28th</time>** and **<time datetime="2026-03-29">Sunday 29th March 2026</time>** at the Church of the Servant King, 33 Dulverton Drive, Milton Keynes MK4 1NA.

## Why take part?

- **FIDE and ECF rated** – ideal if you’re building or chasing a FIDE rating
- **On our doorstep** – same city as the club, minimal travel
- **Four sections** – OPEN, U1900, U1600 and U1400 (ECF), so there’s a section for most strengths
- **Five-round Swiss** over the weekend, 60 minutes per player plus 30 seconds per move
- **Prize fund** £1,160; **entry fee** £40
- **Disability friendly** venue
